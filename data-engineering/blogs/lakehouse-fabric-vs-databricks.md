# Lakehouse Wars: Azure Fabric vs Databricks

Both Azure Fabric and Databricks can support a lakehouse. The right choice depends on your team, scale, skills, and governance needs.

## Azure Fabric

Fabric is attractive when your organization already lives in Microsoft tools. It gives a more integrated experience across OneLake, Power BI, pipelines, and warehouse-style analytics.

## Databricks

Databricks is strong when Spark, Delta Lake, notebooks, machine learning, and engineering-heavy workflows are central to the platform.

## Decision Questions

- Who will operate the platform every day?
- Is the workload mostly BI, heavy engineering, or ML?
- How strict are governance and security requirements?
- What does the team already know?
- What cost model can the business tolerate?

## Takeaway

Choose Fabric when integrated Microsoft analytics matters most. Choose Databricks when flexible engineering depth and Spark-first scale matter most.
